### Workflow 2: One-Step Appointment Booking

<Card title=" ">
  **Scenario:** You just wrapped up a visit and need to book the next one? Skip the clicks—just tell AIR what you need, and it takes care of the scheduling for you. Fast, simple, done.

    

  **What the user says:**

  _“Book a new patient visit for Jimmy Blake, with Dr.Colin Fitzgerald at our 160 Whisman Rd clinic this Friday at 2 PM. Use his primary insurance for the ‘Arm Pain’ case.”_

    

  **What Assistant does:**

  - Verifies patient identity and retrieves her insurance and case info.
  - Checks Dr. Smith’s availability.
  - Books the appointment and links all required information.

  **Benefit:** Eliminates multi-step scheduling tasks and reduces risk of data entry errors.
