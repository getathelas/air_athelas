#### Summary

This report includes primary insurance charges from all claim submissions.

#### Notes

- The provider is based on the rendering provider from the encounter, not the claim submission.
- The insurance information comes from the claim submission, not the encounter. Changes to the encounter’s insurance will not affect the insurance recorded for the claim submission.
- CPT codes are based on those submitted with the claim. Any changes to the procedure codes in the encounter will not affect the CPT codes reported for the claim submission.
- A claim submission must have a submission date to be included in the report.
- The claim submission must be associated with the site to be included.

#### Filters Supported

- Date Range: Date of Service & Date of Submission
- Patients
- Providers
- Facilities
- Insurance Companies
