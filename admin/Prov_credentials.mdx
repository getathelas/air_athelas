Within the Preferences tab from the left navigation bar, you can configure Provider details.

<Card title=" ">
  Update the following details:

  - **Provider Type**
  - **Default Supervising Provider**
  - **Locked Sections** (to restrict editing access for specific Providers)

  ![Prov Creds Web](/images/prov_creds.webp)

  When you click on the **edit** icon you can also edit the Licensing Status, See Direct Access Patients, **Facilities** associated, Custom Signature and **customized Provider Schedule for the week** (including days of the week and timings)
