You can update or manage fax numbers for your site
<Card title="">

- Go to **Insights → Utilities → Faxing → Settings**.
- From here, sites can:
  - **Add new fax numbers** by clicking on the \+ Add Number on the top right of the page. Add the fax number, description, facility, provider, status and fax method for each one.
  - **Edit existing fax numbers** if they change.

<img
  src="/images/New_fax_nu.webp"
  alt="New Fax Nu Web"
  title="New Fax Nu Web"
  style={{ width:"35%" }}
/>

For faxes sent to **external providers**, fax numbers are pulled from the **NPI registry**. If this information is incorrect, sites can **override the auto-pulled number** by entering the correct one manually.

**Note: **Always double-check the provider’s fax number before sending important documents to ensure accuracy.
</Card>
