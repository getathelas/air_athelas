Flowsheets allow you to track interventions, treatments, and evaluative procedures within the Chart Note.

More details: [Flowsheets/Intervention Manager User Guide](https://athelas-public.notion.site/Flowsheets-and-Interventions-v2-237f92a633f28029869efb031cbafc87)

### <Icon icon="notebook" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Fill a Flowsheet**

<Card title>
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Add Treatments" icon="person-walking" titleSize="p">
      **Add Interventions:**

      - Click **Add Interventions** within the Chart Note, or use the Intervention Manager in the right sidebar.
      - Search, filter, and select interventions by:
        - **Type**: Modality, Exercise, Manual Therapy
        - **Body Part**
        - **Category**
        - **CPT Code**
        - **Movement Type**
      - Or use the **search bar** to quickly find interventions by name.
      - Selected interventions will appear in the flowsheet in the order chosen.
      - Drag and drop interventions to change their order. The order is saved and persists across future notes.

      **Note:** Click the **trash icon** to remove an intervention. Interventions marked as **DONE** must be unmarked before deletion.

      ![33 Add Intervention Gi](/images/33_add_intervention.gif)

      **Add Evaluative Procedures:**

      - Click **Add Evaluative Procedure** to select CPT codes not tied to interventions.
      - These appear in the blue header alongside other procedures.
      - Remove evaluative procedures directly if no longer needed.

      <img
        src="/images/evaluative_procedures.webp"
        alt="Evaluative Procedures Web"
        title="Evaluative Procedures Web"
        style={{ width:"51%" }}
      />

      ![34 Add Eval Procedure Gi](/images/34_add_eval_procedure.gif)

      **Add Modalities:**\
      You can create custom interventions for modalities such as ice or hot packs.

      <Danger>
        **✨Smart Tip: Reuse Interventions from Past Visits**

        - Click on clock icon → select a visit date
        - Vew visit-by-visit intervention history
        - Select interventions to re-use values from, then click **Re-Use Selected Rows** to pull data into the current visit.
      </Danger>
      ![32 Add Historic Intervention Gi](/images/32_add_historic_intervention.gif)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Mark Intervention as Done" icon="square-check" titleSize="p">
      - Enter **weights, sets, reps** (free text).
      - Add **treatment duration/minutes** (number input).
      - Use carry-forward icons:
        - **Calendar icon** → carry forward values to next note.
        - **Notes icon** → carry forward weights, sets, reps, and notes.
      - Expand the caret icon for additional fields (e.g., justification, modifiers).
      - Select **CPT Code** (search by name or numeric code). Default values from pre-defined custom interventions (duration, CPT, justification) flow into the flowsheet automatically.
      - Select the provider who performed the intervention (default is **Rendering Provider**)
      - Mark an intervention as **DONE** only after linking it to a CPT code.

      **Note:** Showing modifiers at a per intervention level and justifications can be configured on or off from the Preferences tab.

      ![36 Fill Intervention Gi](/images/36_fill_intervention.gif)

      <Danger>
        ✨ **Smart Tip**: Use **Full-Screen Mode** by clicking the resize icon for easier data entry.
      </Danger>
      ![35 Zoom Screen Gi](/images/35_zoom_screen.gif)
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Card>

### <Icon icon="bookmark" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Mark an Intervention as Concurrent

<Card title=" ">
  In some cases, two interventions may occur at the same time, and you don’t want to bill them separately. In these cases, you can mark interventions as **concurrent**.

  The duration is billed as the **maximum** of the overlapping times, not the sum.

  **Example:** Before: (10 \+ 2 \+ 10 \+ 15 = 37)

  ![Concurrent Before Web](/images/concurrent_before.webp)

  **Example:** After: (10 \+ 2 \+ max(15,10) = 27)

  ![Concurrent After Web](/images/concurrent_after.webp)

  **Prerequisites for marking an intervention as concurrent:**

  - The intervention must be marked as **Done**.
  - Both interventions must have the **same procedure code**.

  Air auto-suggests concurrent options. Selecting one intervention as concurrent automatically maps it back to the other.

  Once you mark Intervention B as concurrent with Intervention A, Intervention A will also be auto-selected with Intervention B (creating a two-way mapping).

  **Note:** You cannot change the CPT code until the concurrent link is removed. You cannot set an intervention to Pending until the concurrent link is removed.
</Card>

### <Icon icon="money-bill-1" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Calculate Units & Time**

<Card title>
  - **Auto-calculate:** The system automatically calculates treatment time and units.
  - **Manual entry:** Turn off auto-calculation toggles if you want to manually enter total time or units (useful for concurrent exercises). You can also add up to four modifiers to a procedure after marking it as done.

  **Note**: In some cases, if two interventions may occur at the same time, you can **mark them as concurrent.**

    

  Users can toggle the visibility of modifiers and justification fields in **Preferences**.

  ![37 Manual Units Time Gi](/images/37_manual_units_time.gif)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="person-walking" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Create & Send a HEP**

<Card title>
  - Mark the checkbox labeled **HEP** (Home Exercise Plan) to select interventions to print.
  - Click the double caret icon to add notes that will appear only in the HEP (not in billing).
  - Click **Print HEP** to preview and send the HEP via email, text, or print as a PDF.

  **Note**: Double click an intervention to mark it as a **To-do** (green border, moves to top)as an exercise to be done today. Reversing a **DONE** intervention to To-Do removes its associated procedure.

  ![Hep Web](/images/hep.webp)

  ![38 Hep Gi](/images/38_hep.gif)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="credit-card" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Create and Edit Custom Interventions

**Create a New Intervention**

<Card title=" ">
  - Navigate to **Interventions** in the left navigation bar.
  - Click **New Intervention**.

  ![New Intervention Pn](/images/new_intervention.png)

  You can specify:

  - The **name** (required) and **instructions** (to be included in the HEP communication to patients).
  - _(Recommended but optional)_ Add a **default CPT code** and **default minutes** for billing.
  - Attach any relevant **images** in the Media tab.
  - Specify the **activity type** (exercise, modality, manual therapy), **category**, **movement**, and **body type**.

  <img
    src="/images/intervention_type_filter.png"
    alt="Intervention Type Filter Pn"
    title="Intervention Type Filter Pn"
    style={{ width:"72%" }}
  />
</Card>

**Create a New Intervention Group**

<Card title=" ">
  - In the Intervention Manager, click **New Group**.
  - Enter a **group name**. Group names are internal only; they do not appear in chart notes or PDFs.
  - _(Optional)_ Assign a **Body Part** or **Movement Type**.
  - Filter interventions by name, body part, or activity type, then click to add them to the right-hand panel.
  - Create **subgroups** as needed and adjust the **order** of interventions or subgroups.

  ![New Intervention Group Web](/images/new_intervention_group.webp)

  <img
    src="/images/select_interventions_group.webp"
    alt="Select Interventions Group Web"
    title="Select Interventions Group Web"
    style={{ width:"50%" }}
  />
</Card>

**Edit an Existing Intervention**

<Card title=" ">
  - Click the intervention’s **name** to open it.
  - Edit details such as instructions, media, CPT code, or default minutes.

  ![Edit Intervention Pn](/images/edit_intervention.png)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="timer" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Review Intervention Progress

<Card title=" ">
  Click **Intervention Progress** in the Flowsheet section to view statistics from all previous interventions.

  - If an intervention was performed in a specific visit, its background will appear grey.
  - The text in each cell represents **weight – sets – reps**.

  ![Intervention Progress Web](/images/intervention_progress.webp)

  Alternate Option: View Intervention progress from [patient’s active case details](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_demog#active-and-discharged-cases)
</Card>
