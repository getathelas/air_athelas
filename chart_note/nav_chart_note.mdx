As you begin a patient consult, set up, complete, and submit a Chart Note.

## Navigate your Chart Note

### <Icon icon="folder-open" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Opening a Chart Note**

<Card title=" ">
  1. Open the **Calendar**.
  2. Select the desired **Appointment**.
  3. Click **View Note** in the top-right corner.
  4. Once opened, patient details appear in the top bar, with Chart Note sections listed in the left sidebar.

  <img
    src="/images/open_chart_note.webp"
    alt="Open Chart Note Web"
    title="Open Chart Note Web"
    style={{ width:"48%" }}
  />

  **Note:** You must check-in the appointment before you can open its Chart Note.

  ![20 View Chart Note Gi](/images/20_view_chart_note.gif)

  Alternate method: [View notes from Patient Profile](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_appts#view-chart-notes-for-an-appointment)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="list-timeline" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Plan of Care**

<Card title>
  When selecting **Plan of Care**, you will be prompted to enter: Start Date and End Date,

  Frequency & Units, Duration & Units, Visit Count

  ![22 Plan Of Care Gi](/images/22_plan_of_care.gif)

  <Danger>
    ✨ **Smart Tip**: If you enter a Start Date, Frequency, and Duration, the system will automatically calculate the End Date and Visit Count.
  </Danger>
</Card>

### <Icon icon="notes-medical" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Other sections in the Chart Note**

<Card title>
  In addition to the Plan of Care, you can view other sections such as:

  - [Measurements](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/other_sec_chart_note#measurements)
  - [Goals](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/other_sec_chart_note#goals)
  - [Treatments](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/other_sec_chart_note#treatments)
  - [**Visits**](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/other_sec_chart_note#visits)
</Card>

## Smart fill your Chart Note

### <Icon icon="file-pen" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Auto-Fill with Athelas AI Scribe**

<Card title>
  More details: [**EHR Scribe v2 User Guide**](https://www.notion.so/athelas-public/EHR-Scribe-v2-User-Guide-1c0f92a633f28065a151d1447b4247f1)

  The Athelas AI Scribe allows you to record a patient consultation and automatically generate detailed documentation.

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Patient Consent for Scribe" icon="file-signature" iconType="duotone" stepNumber={1} titleSize="p">
      Re-confirm patient consent before recording. Patient consent will be taken as part of the Patient Intake Form
    </Step>
    <Step title="Record using Scribe" icon="microphone" iconType="duotone" titleSize="p">
      - Click the blue button in the bottom-right corner to open the AI Widget
      - Select **Scribe**
      - Click **Start Recording**. You can minimize the widget and continue working within the EHR

      ![29 Start Scribe Gi](/images/29_start_scribe.gif)

      - End or cancel the recording when finished

      ![30 Stop Scribe Gi](/images/30_stop_scribe.gif)

      <Danger>
        ✨ **Smart Tip:** While uploading, you can start a new chat on the script to ask questions about the current consultation recording.
      </Danger>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Apply scribe" icon="note-medical" iconType="duotone" titleSize="p">
      - Review the generated content in the Scribe Application Page. Scroll through the Tabs to review all sections
      - Each section includes a checkbox to apply or exclude the scribe output into the Chart Note.
      - Click **Apply Scribe**

      <img
        src="/images/apply_scribe.webp"
        alt="Apply Scribe Web"
        title="Apply Scribe Web"
        style={{ width:"35%" }}
      />

      Note: You can also [lock a section to disable the scribe](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/scribe_faq#lock-sections-from-ai-scribe) for that section.

      <Danger>
        ✨ **Smart Tip**: Use **Reapply Scribe** to restore the most recent recording if you need to revert back. This is helpful if you've edited fields after scribing.
      </Danger>
      Alternate Option: [**Air scribe (mobile app)**](https://athelas-public.notion.site/Air-Scribe-User-Guide-1dcf92a633f28089ad12c6783d5b8ec0)
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Card>

### <Icon icon="message-medical" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Dictate into a section**

<Card title>
  More details: [EHR Dictation v1 User Guide](https://www.notion.so/EHR-Dictation-v1-User-Guide-1c9f92a633f28000a6e7e0517ece550a?pvs=21)

  Once a note is generated, you have the option to add more content through a direct dictation. Dictation allows you to use your voice to enter text directly into a Chart Note.

  - Open Athelas AI and click **Dictate**.
  - Speak normally and watch text appear in real time.
  - Smart punctuation automatically inserts commas, periods, and question marks.
  - Click **Stop** to end dictation.

  ![27 Ai Dictation Gi](/images/27_ai_dictation.gif)
</Card>

<Card title>

## Fill your Flowsheets

More details: [Flowsheets/Intervention Manager User Guide](https://athelas-public.notion.site/Flowsheets-Intervention-Manager-User-Guide-1cdf92a633f280a09b7fc27c23b0e830?pvs=74)

Flowsheets allow you to track interventions, treatments, and evaluative procedures within the Chart Note.

### <Icon icon="notebook" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Filling a Flowsheet**

<Card title>
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Add Treatments" icon="person-walking" titleSize="p">
      **Add Interventions:**

      - Click **Add Interventions** within the Chart Note, or use the Intervention Manager in the right sidebar.
      - Search by body part, treatment, movement, or category.
      - Selected interventions will appear in the flowsheet in the order chosen.

      ![33 Add Intervention Gi](/images/33_add_intervention.gif)

      **Add Evaluative Procedures:**

      Click Add Evaluative Procedure → Select the procedure type and CPT codes → These will appear in the blue header alongside other procedures.

      <img
        src="/images/evaluative_procedures.webp"
        alt="Evaluative Procedures Web"
        title="Evaluative Procedures Web"
        style={{ width:"51%" }}
      />

      ![34 Add Eval Procedure Gi](/images/34_add_eval_procedure.gif)

      **Add Modalities:**\
      You can create custom interventions for modalities such as ice or hot packs.

      <Danger>
        ✨**Smart Tip: Reuse past Interventions** - Click on clock icon → select a visit date → Re-Use Selected Rows
      </Danger>
      ![32 Add Historic Intervention Gi](/images/32_add_historic_intervention.gif)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Mark Intervention as Done" icon="square-check" titleSize="p">
      - Enter weight, sets, and repetitions (free text).
      - Enter treatment duration (in minutes).
      - Expand the intervention using the caret icon to add notes, CPT codes, provider details, justifications, and modifiers.
      - Mark the intervention as done by clicking the checkbox.

      ![36 Fill Intervention Gi](/images/36_fill_intervention.gif)

      **Note**: An intervention cannot be marked as done until it is associated with a CPT Code.

      <Danger>
        ✨ **Smart Tip**: Use **Full-Screen Mode** by clicking the resize icon. This makes flowsheet data entry easier.
      </Danger>
      ![35 Zoom Screen Gi](/images/35_zoom_screen.gif)
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Card>

### <Icon icon="money-bill-1" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Billing Calculations**

<Card title>
  - **Auto-calculate:** The system automatically calculates treatment time and units.
  - **Manual entry:** Turn off auto-calculation toggles if you want to manually enter total time or units (useful for concurrent exercises). You can also add up to four modifiers to a procedure.

  Note: In some cases, if two interventions may occur at the same time, you can [**mark them as concurrent**](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/flowsheet_custom_interventions#mark-an-intervention-as-concurrent)

  ![37 Manual Units Time Gi](/images/37_manual_units_time.gif)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="person-walking" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Create & send a HEP**

<Card title>
  - Mark the checkbox labeled **HEP** (Home Exercise Plan) to select interventions to print.
  - Click the double caret icon to add notes that will appear only in the HEP (not in billing).
  - Preview and send the HEP via email, text, or print as a PDF.

  Note: Double click an intervention to mark it as a **To-do** (exercises to be done today).

  ![Hep Web](/images/hep.webp)

  ![38 Hep Gi](/images/38_hep.gif)
</Card>

## Sign & Submit

### <Icon icon="badge-check" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Compliance Checks with Athelas AI**

<Card title=" ">
  **Compliance checkmarks** ensure that specific details required for compliance are captured in the Chart Note.

  - If the detail is missing, the checkmark appears **grey**.
  - Once the detail is documented, the checkmark turns **green**.

  You can capture this information either by typing it manually or by using the AI Scribe.

  ![Compliance Checkmarks Web](/images/compliance_checkmarks.webp)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="money-check-pen" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Sign a Note**

<Card title>
  The signature section compiles all compliance validations for review. Here, the rendering provider and any co-signers can sign the note.

  <img
    src="/images/notarize_note.webp"
    alt="Notarize Note Web"
    title="Notarize Note Web"
    style={{ width:"53%" }}
  />

  - To fax the encounter to a referring provider, select their name from the dropdown, enter their fax number, and check **Fax note to referring provider**.
  - You may also include a signature request section if needed.
</Card>

### <Icon icon="file-magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  \*\*Preview Chart Note \*\*

<Card title>
  Navigate to **Notarize** within the Chart Note and select **Preview Billing PDF** (next to Fax to Ref. Provider).

  ![41 Preview Pdf Gi](/images/41_preview_pdf.gif)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="check-to-slot" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Submit Encounter**

<Card title>
  - Sign the note under **Notarize** and click **Submit**.
  - If the patient has future appointments scheduled, you can choose to update them with the completed note.
  - Next, you’ll be asked if you’d like to review the appointment in RCM/Billing. Click **View Encounter Details** or **View Claim Details**, or simply click **Complete**.

  <img
    src="/images/submit_note.webp"
    alt="Submit Note Web"
    title="Submit Note Web"
    style={{ width:"53%" }}
  />
</Card>

### <Icon icon="folder-arrow-up" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  **Post Submission**

<Card title=" ">
  Once you sign & submit a Chart Note, you can:

  <Accordion title="Discharge your Patient">
    - **If the patient is in-office: **Change your Chart Note to a Discharge Note to automatically adjust the template and included sections.
    - **If the patient does not visit the office: **Discharge the patient from the [case table within the Patient Profile](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_demog#active-and-discharged-cases)
  </Accordion>
  - **<u>Fax a Chart Note</u>**
  - **<u>View and download updated Note</u>**
</Card>
