In addition to the Plan of Care, Chart Notes include sections for Measurements, Goals, Treatments, and Visits.

### Measurements

<Card title=" ">
  More details: [**Measurements V2 User Guide**](https://athelas-public.notion.site/Measurements-V2-User-Guide-1e5f92a633f2804488c9e44fda548ab2)

  To add new measurements:

  - Open the **Measurements** section in the Chart Note.
  - Click **Manage**.
  - Select the desired measurements and click **Save**.

  You can also:

  - View a patient’s **History of Measurements**.
  - Pull data from the **previous Chart Note** if it exists.

  ![21 Add Measurements Gi](/images/21_add_measurements.gif)

  Measurements are divided into industry-standard types (Observation, Inspection, etc.) and groups.

  <img
    src="/images/measurement_groups.webp"
    alt="Measurement Groups Web"
    title="Measurement Groups Web"
    style={{ width:"82%" }}
  />

  Simply check the boxes for your selected measurements, and click Save to add them to your note.

  <Danger>
    ✨ **Smart Tip**: You can add your own customized [Measurements and Groups in Preferences](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/chart_note_templates#create-custom-preferences)
  </Danger>
  **Note:** AI Scribe will not autofill functional outcome measurements into the Chart Note.
</Card>

### **Goals**

<Card title=" ">
  Goals help providers track patient progress over time. In the Chart Note, the Goals section appears within the **Plan** section of SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan).

  ![23 Goals Gi](/images/23_goals.gif)

  Each goal includes:

  - **Title** – name of the goal.
  - **Description** – details of the goal.
  - **Initial Value (optional)** – patient’s starting state.
  - **Current State (optional)** – updates on progress.
  - **Progress Slider** – visually tracks completion as a percentage.
  - **Goal Tracker** – shows progress over time.

  <img
    src="/images/goal_overall.webp"
    alt="Goal Overall Web"
    title="Goal Overall Web"
    style={{ width:"66%" }}
  />

  **Adding Goals**

  - In an Initial Evaluation note, the Goals section appears blank until goals are added.
  - Click **New Goal** to add one.
  - If goals already exist, click **New Goal** again (top-right) to add more.

  <img
    src="/images/add_goals.webp"
    alt="Add Goals Web"
    title="Add Goals Web"
    style={{ width:"62%" }}
  />

  **Removing Goals**

  - Hover over a goal card.
  - Click the **Delete (trash bin) icon** on the right.
  - Confirm the deletion when prompted.
</Card>

### **Treatments**

<Card title=" ">
  The **Treatments** section allows providers to select treatments tied to corresponding CPT codes.

  ![Treatments Web](/images/treatments.webp)

  <Danger>
    ✨ **Smart Tip**: You can configure your own [Treatment information in Preferences](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/chart_note/chart_note_templates#create-custom-preferences).

</Card>

### **Visits**

<Card title=" ">
  Within the Chart Note, you can track prior authorizations and annual visit limits. These are also visible under patient appointment details. The system will automatically alert providers when authorized or annual visits are running low or nearing expiration.

  ![24 Visits Gi](/images/24_visits.gif)

  Expanding this section provides a detailed view, including:

  - Annual Appointment limits
  - Prior authorization visit limits
  - Remaining Plan of Care visits
  - Remaining Medicare thresholds
  - Progress note upcoming (e.g., every 10 visits, every 45 days)
  - Future appointments scheduled
</Card>
