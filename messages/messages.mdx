The Messages page lets you instantly chat with colleagues or groups (upto 10 people), share files, and even ask Athelas AI.

### <Icon icon="message-plus" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Ask Athelas AI

<Card title=" ">
  <Danger>
    ✨**Smart Tip:** Ask questions to the **Athelas AI**. Conversations will automatically sync with the Athelas AI widget.
  </Danger>
  ![Ai Message Web](/images/ai_message.webp)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="message-quote" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Start a Conversation

<Card title=" ">
  - Go to the **Messages** page.
  - Click the **pencil icon** to start a new thread.
  - Use the search bar to find and select colleagues.
  - Type your message and/or add attachments.

  ![Send Message Pn](/images/send_message.png)
</Card>

### <Icon icon="bell" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Receive Messages

<Card title=" ">
  When you have new messages, notifications will appear in two places:

  - A \*\*red dot \*\*<Icon icon="circle-dot" iconType="duotone" color="#ec0b0b" /> on the notification center (bell icon) in the header of any page.
  - A \*\*blue dot \*\*<Icon icon="circle-dot" iconType="duotone" color="#1a5bee" /> on the **Messages** page itself.
