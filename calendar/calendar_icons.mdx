### Appointment Stages

<Card title=" ">
  As appointments progress through different stages, their blocks update visually. This helps you:

  - Identify canceled appointments at a glance
  - Focus on active appointments (completed ones fade automatically)

### Appointment Icons and Tooltip

<Card title=" ">
  Hover over any Calendar block to view a tooltip.

  You can switch between **Condensed** and **Expanded** views by clicking on the caret icons.

  The tooltip displays the most helpful information first, including Case and Appointment Type, Eligibility Status and Insurance, Prior Authorization Status, Future Visits Scheduled and Appointment Status.
