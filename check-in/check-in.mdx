At the time of consultation, you can check in your patient and prepare for the visit.

### Automatic check-in with Athelas AI

<Card title=" ">
  <Danger>
    ✨**Smart Tip:** Go to **Athelas AI** → Enter your prompt → Athelas AI will check in the patient and open their chart note and/or patient details automatically.
  </Danger>
  ![9 Ai Checkin Gi](/images/9_ai_checkin.gif)
</Card>

### Manual check-in

<Card title=" ">
  Open the **Calendar** → Click on the desired **Appointment** → Select **Check-in** for the patient.

  You can also update the appointment status to **Completed, No Show, Canceled,** or **Archived**.

  ![8 Checkin Options Gi](/images/8_checkin_options.gif)

  **Alternate method**: Navigate to **Patients** tab in the left navigation bar → Open the **Appointments** Tab → Select **Check in** (black square icon)

  **Note:** To check in an Appointment a [**<u>Case Type</u>**](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/check-in/edit_appt) must be present. If no case is associated, you will receive an error when attempting to check in.
</Card>
