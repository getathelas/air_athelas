At the time of consultation, you can check in your patient and prepare for the visit.

### <Icon icon="comment-check" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Automatic check-in with Athelas AI

<Card title=" ">
  <Danger>
    ✨**Smart Tip:** Go to **Athelas AI** → Enter your prompt → Athelas AI will check in the patient and open their chart note and/or patient details automatically. You can also use Voice Mode.
  </Danger>
  ![9 Ai Checkin Gi](/images/9_ai_checkin.gif)

  **Note**: To check in an Appointment a [Case Type](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/check-in/edit_appt) must be present. If no case is associated, you will receive an error when attempting to check in.

  **Note:** When you open the Chart Note, you may have Visit alerts that are required to be addressed before you progress.
</Card>

### <Icon icon="clipboard-check" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Manual check-in

<Card title=" ">
  Open the **Calendar** → Click on the desired **Appointment** → Select **Check-in** for the patient on the Tooltip.

    

  You can also update the appointment status to **Completed, No Show, Canceled,** or **Archived**.

  **Note**: You can also "uncheck-in" the patient if checked-in by mistake.

  ![8 Checkin Options Gi](/images/8_checkin_options.gif)

  **Alternate method**: Navigate to **Patients** tab in the left navigation bar → Open the **Appointments** Tab → Select **Check in** (black square icon)
</Card>
