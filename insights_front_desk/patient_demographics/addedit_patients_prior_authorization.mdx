---
title: "Add/edit Patient's Prior Authorization"
---

Within the Patient Demographics section, to add Prior Authorization, scroll down and click “+ New Prior Auth” under the Prior Authorization section

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/addedit_patients_prior_authorization/addedit_patients_prior_authorization_1.png" alt="" />

When you click “+ New Prior Auth”, a small window will open up in the middle of your screen. Here you can enter the following fields:

- Auth Number
- Effective Date
- Expiration Date
- Total Number of Visits
- Insurance this Prior Auth is tied to:

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/addedit_patients_prior_authorization/addedit_patients_prior_authorization_2.png" alt="" />

Click “+Create” once you have entered all the desired information

Also as Faxes come in, you can categorize the fax as a Prior Authorization or Referrals:

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/addedit_patients_prior_authorization/addedit_patients_prior_authorization_3.png" alt="" />

**Features In Development**

- Prior authorization tracking by CPT codes and units, auto-update auth workflows, support unit-based authorizations in the working list, and integrate seamlessly into claim submission
- Send outbound referrals
