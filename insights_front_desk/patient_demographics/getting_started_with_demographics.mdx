#### For viewing existing patient demographics

Navigate to Patients tab within the Navigation bar, and search for your patient's name here

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics_1.png" alt="" />

You are now in the patient’s demographics. Click on the below sections to add or edit information

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics_2.png" alt="" />

SSN is hidden, but can be viewed by selecting they "eye" icon

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics_3.png" alt="" />

#### Adding new patient information

Select "Create New Patient" from the Patients tab

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics/getting_started_with_demographics_4.png" alt="" />

A side panel will open up, here you will be able to enter in the general patient demographics information. Please fill in the following required fields:

- Full Patient Name

- First and Last Name
- Middle Name (If Applicable)

- Gender
- Date of Birth
- Contact Information

- Home Address (Address Line(s), City, State, Post Code and Country Code)
- Mailing Address (If Different from Home Address)
- Phone Number
- Email Address

- You can also create a new patient from the Calendar directly
