The [Air Patient Portal](https://portal.athelas.com/) offers convenient self-service options for patients to manage their healthcare experience. These sections will walk you through the main features available to patients.

#### How to Connect a Patient with their Patient Portal

Navigate to the patient’s profile.

Click the smartphone icon to send a registration link to that patient.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_communications/getting_started_with_patient_portal/getting_started_with_patient_portal_1.png" alt="" />

Patients will be invited to sign up for the portal after making payments or completing forms. They can also access their existing patient portal by signing in at [portal.athelas.com](http://portal.athelas.com/).
