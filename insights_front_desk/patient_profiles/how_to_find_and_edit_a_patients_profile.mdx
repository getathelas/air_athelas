##### ⚠️ Important Note

**Only a patient’s contact and mailing information to be updated in Insights. If you need to edit a patient’s basic identifying information**, like name or date of birth, you can do so in your EHR, or contact [support@getathelas.com](mailto:support@getathelas.com). Athelas will reflect those changes in Insights within 24 hours.

### How to Find a Patient's Profile

#### The Speedy Way

From any page in your Athelas Insights interface, you can use the **Universal Search** feature. Here’s how to access it:

You can either click the 🔍 icon in the page header…

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_1.png" alt="" />

…or you can press **Cmd + K** on a Mac keyboard (**Ctrl + K** for PC users).

Either way, a universal search window will appear.

Simply start typing a patient’s name and select their Patient Profile link, like so:

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_2.png" alt="" />

**Pro tip**: Universal Search (shortcut Cmd + K for Mac, or Ctrl + K for PC users) can search for anything in Insights — specific appointments, payment plans, claims, you name it. Use it well and often.

#### The Classic Way

Navigate to the [Patient Responsibility](https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility) tab. Click into the ‘Patient’ search field and enter the name of the patient.

**Click the patient name** where it appears in the list to enter the patient’s account.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_3.png" alt="" />

This will take you to the patient’s profile.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_4.png" alt="" />

####

#### Editing Patient Profile Information

To change the patient’s email address, phone number, or statement mailing address, click the ‘Actions’ menu and choose ‘Edit.’

*As previously mentioned, basic identifying information like patient name and home address cannot be edited here. Update that information in your EHR first and it will appear in Insights within 24 hours.*

A new window will appear. Update the fields you would like to change and then click `Update`.

Note that you may need to refresh the page afterward for your changes to appear.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_5.png" alt="" />

You can now see the patient’s updated information in their profile header card.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_6.png" alt="" />

Use the top three buttons to [add or update the patient’s credit cards on file](https://athelas.helpkit.so/how-to-guides/mx4v4WBLUG3MERujomrLhL/how-to-save-not-save-add-or-remove-credit-cards-for-future-payments/8zH7FTHDnCi3Mq9TLkkEiG), [write a note](https://athelas.helpkit.so/how-to-guides/mx4v4WBLUG3MERujomrLhL/how-to-use-standard-or-universal-notes/xciEPrKBvQ1ViTQB7P4wMH) about the patient, [download their current mail statement](https://athelas.helpkit.so/how-to-guides/mx4v4WBLUG3MERujomrLhL/how-to-print-a-patient-statement/aGR2oKQBeFT6k51tJZga7j), or create a patient portal link for this patient.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_profiles/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile/how_to_find_and_edit_a_patients_profile_7.png" alt="" />

**Transaction History**

View all transactions in a chronological history here. You can also click in to individual transactions to find receipts or make refunds, just as you would in the Charges tab.

**Eligibility**

Full eligibility details available here, along with raw EDI info if you click `See More Details`. See the guide on [how to run eligibility checks](https://athelas.helpkit.so/how-to-guides/mx4v4WBLUG3MERujomrLhL/%E2%80%8D-how-to-run-an-eligibility-check/1x4H8JSdeXCv1QegVUqLir) for more information.

**Touchpoints**

All automated PR collection texts and emails are listed here, along with data on their success or lack thereof. You can update the content and frequency of these automatic communications in [PR Settings](https://athelas.helpkit.so/overviews/eWPj3tstxRFRmL2WdFA4cV/pr-settings/wAZezZYGNVT9Go7veZGyNC).

**Claims**

All of this patient’s claims are listed here. This is functionally the same as using this patient’s name as a filter on the [Claim Details page](https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view).

**Prior Authorization**

View, create, and edit prior authorizations here.

You’ll generally come here during patient intake, or when claims are denied due to an issue with prior auth and you need to update this information.

See a walkthrough example of working a denial in this Denials Worklist page [guide](https://athelas.helpkit.so/overviews/eWPj3tstxRFRmL2WdFA4cV/denials-worklist-page/49C2Ycoa2CQ6qb8KbxxesP).

**Documents**

You can store a patient’s documents for future reference in this tab, including insurance card, intake information, driver’s license, and more.
