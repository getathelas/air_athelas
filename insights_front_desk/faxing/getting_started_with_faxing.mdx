---
title: "Getting Started with Faxing"
---

There is a dedicated faxing page within Air to send and receive faxes. To get to this page click on *Utilities* on the left hand menu, then select the *Faxing* section.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/faxing/getting_started_with_faxing/getting_started_with_faxing_1.png" alt="" />

Within the section, you have several tabs:

- To Be Sent - where you can store faxes ready to send
- Outbound - faxes sent
- Inbound - faxes received
- Archived - archive any fax in queue, outbound or inbound status
- Settings - where fax numbers are stored

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/faxing/getting_started_with_faxing/getting_started_with_faxing_2.png" alt="" />

Create, edit, and tag contacts, link numbers to facilities and providers under "Contact List"

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/faxing/getting_started_with_faxing/getting_started_with_faxing_3.png" alt="" />

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/faxing/getting_started_with_faxing/getting_started_with_faxing_4.png" alt="" />

And can filter faxes by Facility, Provider and Status
