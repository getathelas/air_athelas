### At a Glance

Need to just send a quick message to a colleague or have a conversation better suited to an instant messaging system than an email thread? Head over to the [Messages page](https://insights.athelas.com/messages)! This feature enables staff to interact with each other, all within Insights itself. Up to ten staff can be added to a thread.

### Here’s How it Works

To begin a thread with one or more colleagues, navigate to the Messages page and click the pencil icon to create a new conversation.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/messaging/messages_page/messages_page_1.png" alt="" />

A search bar will appear. Search for the colleague in question and select their name when it appears.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/messaging/messages_page/messages_page_2.png" alt="" />

Now, you can write out a message and/or add attachments, as shown below.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/messaging/messages_page/messages_page_3.png" alt="" />

### Received Messages

When there are new messages for you, notifications will appear in two places.

As a red dot 🔴 on the notification center (🔔 icon) in the header of any page in Insights.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/messaging/messages_page/messages_page_4.png" alt="" />

As a blue dot 🔵 on the Messages page.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/messaging/messages_page/messages_page_5.png" alt="" />

#### Notes

You can also leave notes on a given encounter outside of the messages tab:

**Features Supported:**

- Leave notes on a given encounter
- Tag users in a note
- Leave universal notes (note will show throughout Insights for that patient - all encounters, claims, appointments, etc)
**Features Not Supported:**

- Clearly notify a user if they are tagged in a note
