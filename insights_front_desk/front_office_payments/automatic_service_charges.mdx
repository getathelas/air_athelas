Athelas lets you calculate patient's service charges, post encounter, using specific CPT codes instead of just general appointment types, factoring in patient benefits, deductibles, and real-time eligibility to provide accurate estimates of what patients should pay before they leave your practice.

### <Icon icon="circle-plus" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Adding Service Charges to an Appointment


<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/front_office_payments/automatic_service_charges/automatic_service_charges_1.png" alt="" />

You can add service items to any appointment, and the system will calculate the charges based on your configured CPT rates.

**To add service charges:**

1. Navigate to the **Appointments** page and open the appointment
2. Click on **Charges**
3. Click **Add Service Item**
4. The system calculates charges based on:
   - Your CPT charge rates
   - Patient's insurance benefits
   - Current deductible status
   - Eligibility information

<Note>
The calculation uses specific CPT codes and factors in patient benefits, deductibles, and real-time eligibility to provide accurate estimates of what patients should pay before they leave your practice.
</Note>

### <Icon icon="gear" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Configuring Service Charge Rates

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/front_office_payments/automatic_service_charges/automatic_service_charges_2.png" alt="" />

Service charge rates are configured in your practice settings and determine the base amounts used for calculations.

**To configure CPT charge rates:**

1. Go to **My Practice**
2. Navigate to **PR Settings**
3. Navigate to **Service Charges** (located at the bottom of the settings menu)
4. Add or update your CPT codes and their corresponding charge amounts

### FAQ

<Accordion title="What information does the calculation consider?">
The calculation considers:

- CPT charge rates configured in your Service Charges settings
- Patient's current insurance eligibility and benefits
- Deductible amounts (remaining or already met)
- Out-of-pocket maximums
- Appointment type and services rendered

This provides a more accurate estimate than appointment-based charges alone.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I view the calculated charges?">
Calculated charges appear in the **Charges** section of the appointment details page. The system shows both the base charge amount and the estimated patient responsibility based on their insurance benefits.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I still manually adjust charges if needed?">
Yes, you can manually adjust charges as needed. The calculation provides an initial estimate, but you maintain full control to modify charges based on specific circumstances or documentation.
</Accordion>
