title: "Setting up a Payment Plan"
---

#### Here’s How to Do It

From the [Patient Responsibility](https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility) page, click on the Actions menu and choose Set Up Payment Plan.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/setting_up_a_payment_plan/setting_up_a_payment_plan_1.png" alt="" />

In the popup, search for the patient in question.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/setting_up_a_payment_plan/setting_up_a_payment_plan_2.png" alt="" />

Fill in start date and frequency of payments, max amount per charge, and choose a credit card or add a new card.

You’ll also have the opportunity to use outstanding balance and/or to set a fixed amount per payment.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/setting_up_a_payment_plan/setting_up_a_payment_plan_3.png" alt="" />

Click `Set Up Payment Plan`, and you’re done! You can now see a Payment Plan indicator on the patient’s listing, under their Current Balance.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/setting_up_a_payment_plan/setting_up_a_payment_plan_4.png" alt="" />
