---

**Quick Version: If you still have the incorrect write-off in front of you:**

- Click the ‘Write Off’ button again to open the write-off controls.
- Then click the red ‘Remove Write Off’ button and you’re all set.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/how_to_undo_a_write_off/how_to_undo_a_write_off_1.png" alt="" />

Otherwise, see the detailed guide below for instructions.

## How to Undo a Write-off

Navigate to the patient’s profile.

In the ‘Charges tab (or Transaction History tab), find the line with the written-off charges you want to reverse and click ‘View Details.’

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/how_to_undo_a_write_off/how_to_undo_a_write_off_2.png" alt="" />

Click ‘Write-Off.’

Yes, really. This will open a window that gives you the option to undo the write-off.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/how_to_undo_a_write_off/how_to_undo_a_write_off_3.png" alt="" />

In the popup, fill in the amount you would like to reverse and the reason for the reversal, then click ‘Remove Write Off.’

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/how_to_undo_a_write_off/how_to_undo_a_write_off_4.png" alt="" />

### Write-Off Reversed!

The patient’s PR for this claim will now return to its original state, and the patient’s outstanding balance will be updated across Athelas Insights to reflect your action.

<img src="/images/insights_front_desk/patient_responsibility/how_to_undo_a_write_off/how_to_undo_a_write_off_5.png" alt="" />
