### <Icon icon="chart-line-up" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  My Reports

<Card title=" ">
  Air by Athelas has reporting available under the Insights tab on the left hand side.

  An overview of KPIs can be found in Insights -\> Performance Analysis.  This can be filtered by facility and by provider.

   The rovides actionable insights into the performance of your practice. It highlights key statistics that help you monitor and evaluate operational efficiency, patient outcomes, and financial success.

  These reports can be downloaded.  As an example, there is a summary metric for under-scheduled patients, where patients have fewer appointments than their plan of care.  Metrics also show trends over time. 

  Tracks core metrics such as active patients, appointment totals, provider efficiency, and appointment breakdown by type and status. 

  Each metric is summarized in a card with a “last period” comparison to flag positive or negative trends.\
  _“Last Period”_ refers to the previous interval equal to the selected date range (e.g., if you select 7 days, “last period” means 8–14 days ago).

  Apply filters by Date Range, Facilities, and Providers. These apply to most cards, downloads, and tables, with exceptions such as Active, Scheduled, and Dropped Patients, which depend on case status rather than date.

  Each card includes a download option to export raw data as a CSV. These files include details such as appointment IDs and patient contact information to help with follow-up.

  \
  Clicking a card opens a table with detailed data. For example, the “Underscheduled Patients” card shows patient IDs, names, Plan of Care details, and progress percentages. Each patient ID links directly to the patient’s EHR profile.

  ![Kpi Dashboard Web](/images/kpi_dashboard.webp)

  **Note:** Ask Athelas AI for quick reports and statistics.

  \
  Appointment Total cards include their own dropdown filters so you can quickly view appointment metrics by status.

  The “Provider Efficiency” card shows the percentage of completed appointments compared to a benchmark (currently 80 appointments per week per provider, soon to be configurable).

  | **Card**                          | **Description**                                                                                              |
  | :-------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
  | **Active Patients**               | Number of patients with an open case (not archived or discharged).                                           |
  | **Scheduled Patients**            | Patients with an open case and at least one future appointment scheduled.                                    |
  | **Dropped Patients**              | Patients with an open case but no future appointments scheduled.                                             |
  | **Underscheduled Patients**       | Patients with fewer completed/checked-in visits than expected in their Plan of Care (scheduling ratio \< 1). |
  | **Average POC Scheduling Ratio**  | The average scheduling ratio across all patients with a valid Plan of Care.                                  |
  | **Discharges**                    | Patients discharged with their case closed during the selected timeframe.                                    |
  | **Unsigned Notes**                | Total number of unsigned notes, plus count of notes signed during the selected period.                       |
  | **Arrival Rate**                  | Percentage of scheduled appointments that were checked in or completed.                                      |
  | **No Show Rate**                  | Percentage of scheduled appointments marked as “No Show.”                                                    |
  | **Cancellation Rate**             | Percentage of scheduled appointments that were cancelled.                                                    |
  | **Provider Efficiency**           | Completed/checked-in appointments compared to a benchmark (80 per week per provider, soon configurable).     |
  | **Appointment Count**             | Total number of appointments, filterable by status (default = Scheduled).                                    |
  | **Average Frequency of Visit**    | Average visits per week per active patient, calculated over a rolling 4-week period.                         |
  | **Initial Evaluations Scheduled** | Number of scheduled or completed appointments of the “Initial Evaluation” type.                              |
</Card>
