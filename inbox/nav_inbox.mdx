### <Icon icon="envelope-open-text" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  My Inbox

<Card title=" ">
  The Inbox is your home for understanding what notes need to be signed and submitted.

  Each row in the left navigation is a note - shows patient name, date of service, and clinical note type. Clicking on the row will open the note on the right.

</Card>

### <Icon icon="chart-line-up" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  My Reports

<Card title=" ">
  Air by Athelas has reporting available under the Insights tab on the left hand side.

  An overview of KPIs can be found in Insights -\> Performance Analysis.  This can be filtered by facility and by provider.

  These reports can be downloaded.  As an example, there is a summary metric for under-scheduled patients, where patients have fewer appointments than their plan of care.  Metrics also show trends over time.
</Card>
