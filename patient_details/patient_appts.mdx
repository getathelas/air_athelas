**Import Records for Past Appointments**

<Card title=" ">
  **Context:**

  - Plan of Care details will not be migrated from the old EHR into Athelas Air.
  - For returning patients seen in Air for the first time, providers will need to reference their previous documentation and set up the Plan of Care in Air.
  - This is a one-time step: once entered, the Plan of Care will automatically carry forward from note to note.

  **Steps:**

  - Open the recent-most Progress note / Initial Eval for the patient on your old EHR which has the Plan of Care.
  - Find that appt on Athelas Air by entering the Attachment’s tab of the Patient’s Profile
  - Click the **\+ icon** to the right of the appointment → **Set Up Chart Note**.
  - Select the right **Case, Appointment Type,** and **Clinical Note Type** [Progress Note, Initial Evaluation, etc.]
  - Select **For Documentation Only** when uploading. This option ensures the imported chart note is **not billable**.
  - Upload the document.
  - Type in the Plan of Care from your Old EHR into the Air Chart Note.
  - You will now see the correct Plan of Care visits moving forward in future Chart Notes made on Air.

  **Note**: By uploading a PDF of a past visit chart note from your previous EHR - you will now be able to view and edit their chart note details within Air.

  ![17 Import Records Gi](/images/17_import_records.gif)
</Card>
