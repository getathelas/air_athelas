---
title: "View your Patient's Information"
---

Once you check in a patient, you can view past visit summaries and detailed patient information.

## View a Summary of Past Visits
   ### <Icon icon="message-lines" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> AI-Generated Summary

    <Card title=" ">
      <Danger>
        ✨**Smart Tip:** Scroll through the _Subjective_ section of the Chart Note to review an AI-generated summary of previous appointments.
      </Danger>
      ![19 Ai Summary2 Gi](/images/19_ai_summary2.gif)
    </Card>
    <Accordion title="Alternate method: Review past visit Chart Notes">
      - Open **Chart Notes**.
      - Click on the **Appointments** tab at the top.
      - Click on the **square icon** (to the right of the calendar icon) to open the Chart Note for the desired appointment.

      **Note**: From this page, you can also view past [Billing and Plan of Care](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_appts#preview-past-billing-and-plan-of-care-documents) documents by clicking on the "eye" icon to the left of the date.

      ![Pt Appt Past Web](/images/pt_appt_past.webp)
    </Accordion>
 
## Review Patient Details
    ### <Icon icon="magnifying-glass-plus" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Ask Athelas AI

    <Card title=" ">
      <Danger>
        ✨**Smart Tip:** Use Athelas AI to ask targeted questions about the patient for quick insights.
      </Danger>
    </Card>
   ### <Icon icon="image-user" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  Open Patient Information Tabs

    <Card title=" ">
      Use the tabs at the top of the Chart Note to navigate patient information, including:

      - [Patient Demographics](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_demog)
      - [Patient Appointment Details](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_appts)
      - [Patient Documents](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_attachments) (including faxes and images)
      - Tasks, Medications, Allergies, Visits, Immunizations and Labs

      ![Patient Info Chart Notes Web](/images/patient_info_chart_notes.webp)

      <Accordion title="Alternate method: Open Patient Profile">
        Click on the **Patient** tab in the left menu → Search and select your patient by entering their details.

        ![Pt Tab Web](/images/pt_tab.webp)

        This opens the patient’s full profile for review.

        ![Pt Appt Past Web](/images/pt_appt_past.webp)
      </Accordion>
    </Card>
