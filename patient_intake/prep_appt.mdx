  Click any appointment block to open its side panel. From here you can:
  - Edit the appointment (pencil icon)
  - Change status (Checked In, Cancelled, No Show, Completed)
  - Add a follow-up
  - Delete the appointment
  - Email the patient
  - View demographics (click the patient name)
  - Track related items (Prior Auth, Eligibility, Balances, Chart Note status, etc.)

  ### Encounter Details

  <Card title>
    The **Encounter Details** page (under Daily Operations) shows all encounters and their statuses (Open, Closed, In Progress, Blocked, Archived). From here you can bulk update statuses or edit encounter details.
  </Card>
  ### Reminders & Intake Forms

  <Card title>
    From the appointment details, click **Send** next to Reminders/Forms.
    - Select individual forms or workflows (groups of forms).
    - Send via text, email, or both.
    - Override the patient’s contact info temporarily if needed.

    💡 **Workflows** automate which forms/reminders are sent, to whom, and when.
  </Card>
  ### Prior Auth & Visit Alerts

  <Card title>
    Air will automatically alert you when prior authorizations or visit counts are nearing expiration/limits.
    - **Orange alert** = nearing expiration
    - **Red alert** = expired or no visits left

    These details can be edited directly in the appointment.

    **Prior Authorization List**

    In Encounter Details, click the checkmark list icon to view all prior authorizations (referrals and pre-certifications). Results can be filtered, downloaded, edited, or deleted.
  </Card>
  ### Eligibility Checks

  <Card title>
    Air runs eligibility checks one week and one day before each appointment. Results are updated to **Active** or **Inconclusive.** You can re-run checks anytime from the appointment details.
  </Card>
  ### Patient Balances

  <Card title>
    View and charge outstanding balances from the appointment details. Clicking **Charge** routes you to the Daily Operations \> Appointments page to finalize payment.
  </Card>
  ### Referring Providers

  <Card title>
    Referring providers can be added, viewed, or edited directly in appointment details. Use **\+ Add Provider** to search by name/NPI or create a new provider.
  </Card>

## Send an Intake Form

<Card title>
  - Create custom intake forms under **Utilities \> Templates.** Drag/drop components (e.g., Single Choice, Text) to build forms.
  - Save as **Patient Intake Templates.**
  - Automate form delivery through **Automation \> Patient Flow.**
  - Configure by appointment type, patient, provider, facility, insurance, or age.
  - Set reminders to send immediately or at chosen intervals.
</Card>

You will be prompted to add a cancellation reason which will be available to view on the patient’s profile under the appointments tab. More information can be found here: [Patients](https://www.notion.so/Patients-158f92a633f280f8b633e676de30c656?pvs=21)

Adding an annual visit limit for a new service type will be done here by clicking to the same pencil icon. You’ll be able to select the service type and even include a custom plan year start date- meaning you’ll start the year from a unique date instead of January 1st of that year

Alternately, you are also able to create a patient directly while you’re creating an appointment. This is useful when a new walk-in patient comes into the clinic. To do so, click on the person icon to the right of the _Patient_ field when creating a new appointment. A new side panel will open up on the right with the exact same fields for you to fill out as the above.

**”Use case**: The data import process will carry over past appointments, but opening the chart note in Air will not yield any details. By uploading a PDF of their chart note from your previous EHR- you will now be able to view and edit their chart note details within Air.

Furthermore, you can easily bulk fax and download PDFs by clicking on the checkbox at the very top of the appointments table. All of the completed appointments will have a checkbox next to them. You’ll be able to uncheck any that you don’t want to download or simply check the boxes next to the appointments you’d like to download or fax.💡 Two forms—**Patient Update** and **Insurance Update**—are hard-coded with write-back capabilities. These auto-update demographics when submitted.
