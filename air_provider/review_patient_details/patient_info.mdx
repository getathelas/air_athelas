Once you check in a patient, you can view past visit summaries and detailed patient information.

### <Icon icon="note" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} />  View a Summary of Past Visits

**AI-Generated Summary**

<Card title=" ">
  <Danger>
    ✨**Smart Tip:** Scroll through the _Subjective_ section of the Chart Note to review an AI-generated summary of previous appointments.
  </Danger>
  ![19 Ai Summary2 Gi](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_3.gif)
</Card>

<Accordion title="Alternate method: Review past visit Chart Notes">
  - Open **Chart Notes**.
  - Click on the **Appointments** tab at the top.
  - Click on the **square icon** (to the right of the calendar icon) to open the Chart Note for the desired appointment.

  **Note**: From this page, you can also view past [Billing and Plan of Care](https://air_athelas.mintlify.app/patient_details/patient_appts#preview-past-billing-and-plan-of-care-documents) documents by clicking on the "eye" icon to the left of the date.

  ![Pt Appt Past Web](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp)
</Accordion>

<Card title=" ">

  ![3 Ptinfo Gi](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_3.gif)

**Open Patient Information Tabs**

<Card title=" ">
  <Danger>
    ✨**Smart Tip:** Ask Athelas AI to navigate you to the relevant Patient Profile or Chart Note. You can also use Voice Mode.
  </Danger>
  ![4 Nav Ptpage Gi](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_3.gif)

  Use the tabs at the top of the Chart Note to navigate patient information, including:

  - [Patient Demographics](https://trainings.air.athelas.com/patient_details/patient_demog)
  - [Patient Appointment Details](https://trainings.air.athelas.com/patient_details/patient_appts)
  - [Patient Documents](https://trainings.air.athelas.com/patient_details/patient_attachments) (including faxes and images)
  - Tasks, Medications, Allergies, Visits, Immunizations and Labs

  ![Patient Info Chart Notes Web](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp)

  <Accordion title="Alternate method: Open Patient Profile">
    **Note**: Use Athelas AI to navigate to the Patient Profile

    Click on the **Patient** tab in the left menu → Search and select your patient by entering their details.

    ![Pt Tab Web](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp)

    This opens the patient’s full profile for review.

    ![Pt Appt Past Web](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp)
  </Accordion>
</Card>

### Add Patient Notes

<Card title>
  There are 3 types of notes:

  **1. Appointment Notes**

  View Calendar \> Click on an appointment \> Edit appointment to enter any notes. 

  <img
    src="/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp"
    alt="New Appt Web"
    title="New Appt Web"
    style={{ width:"51%" }}
  />

  These notes can be seen in i) appointment details, ii) the calendar tooltip.

  <img
    src="/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.jpeg"
    alt="Appt Notes2 Jpe"
    title="Appt Notes2 Jpe"
    style={{ width:"69%" }}
  />

  **2. Case Notes**

  View Patient Profile \> Patient Demographics \> Edit Active Cases to enter any case specific notes.

  ![Update Case Owner Prov Web](/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp)

  **3. Patient Notes**

  Patient notes are of 2 types:

  a) General Notes: View Patient Profile \> Patient Demographics \> Add Patient Notes. These notes can be seen in the appointment details or within patient demographics.

  b) Pinned Notes: When enabled within the Preferences Tab, pinned notes will open automatically when the patient's profile or appointments or chart notes are opened.

  <img
    src="/images/air_provider/review_patient_details/patient_info/patient_info_1.webp"

  />
</Card>
