Functional Outcome Assessment helps you document whether a patient has completed a functional outcome measure and (when needed) whether a **plan of care** was documented on time.

Use this workflow inside the patient’s **Visit Note** to ensure the measure is counted correctly as **Performance Met**, **Performance Not Met**, or an **Exception**.

## Complete the assessment in the visit note

### <Icon icon="clipboard-check" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Add Functional Outcome Assessment

**To add the assessment:**

1. Open the patient’s **Visit Note**.
2. Go to **Assessment**.
3. Add **Functional Outcome Assessment**.

![Functional Outcome Assessment section in the visit note](/images/air_provider/fill_a_chart_note/functional_outcome_assessment/functional_outcome_assessment_1.webp)

### <Icon icon="timer" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Answer the “completed in the last 30 days” question

When the assessment was completed for this patient within the past **30 days** of the appointment date, the first question will be **True** and you don’t need to repeat it.

If the first question is **False**, you must add and complete a functional outcome form for this visit.

### <Icon icon="clipboard" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Add and complete a functional outcome form (if needed)

**To add a form:**

1. In the **Visit Note**, go to **Objective** → **Functional Outcomes**.
2. Add any functional outcome form **except** **PHQ-9**.
3. Have the patient complete the form.

Once the form is completed, the second question in **Functional Outcome Assessment** becomes **True**.

### <Icon icon="circle-dot" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Mark whether a deficiency was identified

After the form is completed, indicate whether a functional outcome deficiency was identified:

- If **no deficiency** was identified, set **Functional Outcome deficiency identified** to **False**.
- If a **deficiency** was identified, set **Functional Outcome deficiency identified** to **True**.

![Functional Outcome deficiency identified question](/images/air_provider/fill_a_chart_note/functional_outcome_assessment/functional_outcome_assessment_2.webp)

## If a deficiency is identified: document plan of care

When you mark **Functional Outcome deficiency identified** as **True**, you must indicate whether a plan of care was documented within **2 days**.

### <Icon icon="box-circle-check" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Plan of care documented within 2 days (Performance Met)

If the plan of care was documented within 2 days, mark the plan of care question as **True**. This counts as **Performance Met**.

![Plan of care documented within 2 days set to True](/images/air_provider/fill_a_chart_note/functional_outcome_assessment/functional_outcome_assessment_3.webp)

### <Icon icon="circle-exclamation" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> Patient not eligible for care plan (Exception)

If the plan of care was **not** documented within 2 days and the patient is **not eligible** for a care plan, mark **Patient not eligible for care plan** as **True**. This counts as an **Exception**.

![Patient not eligible for care plan exception](/images/air_provider/fill_a_chart_note/functional_outcome_assessment/functional_outcome_assessment_4.webp)

### <Icon icon="xmark" iconType="duotone" color="#F9345F" size={23} /> If neither is selected (Performance Not Met)

If the plan of care question is **False** and you don’t mark the patient as not eligible for a care plan, the measure counts as **Performance Not Met**.

![Example of Performance Not Met state](/images/air_provider/fill_a_chart_note/functional_outcome_assessment/functional_outcome_assessment_5.webp)

## Exceptions

You may see exception scenarios in the Functional Outcome Assessment workflow:

- **Patient not eligible to participate**: If a form wasn’t completed because the patient is not eligible to participate, mark **Patient not eligible to participate** as **True** (this is an **Exception**).
- **Patient not eligible for care plan**: If a deficiency was identified but the patient is not eligible for a care plan, mark **Patient not eligible for care plan** as **True** (this is an **Exception**).

### FAQ

<Accordion title="When do I need to add a new functional outcome form?">
  If the first question (completed in the past **30 days**) is **False**, you need to add and complete a functional outcome form for the visit so the second question can become **True**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which functional outcome forms can I use?">
  You can use any functional outcome form available in **Objective** → **Functional Outcomes**, **except** **PHQ-9**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the difference between the two exception options?">
  - **Patient not eligible to participate**: used when the patient can’t complete the functional outcome form.
  - **Patient not eligible for care plan**: used when a deficiency is identified but a care plan is not applicable.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I leave the care plan eligibility question blank when the plan of care was not documented within 2 days?">
  If the plan of care question is **False** and you don’t mark the patient as not eligible for a care plan, the measure is counted as **Performance Not Met**.
</Accordion>
