Claim denials related to modifiers are one of the top resolvable denials we've seen across the providers we work with. When you're focused on delivering quality patient care, it's easy to overlook the complex modifier requirements associated with Medicare reimbursements.

This is especially true for the KX modifier, a two-character code used to indicate a particular service or therapy is medically necessary and exceeds the annual threshold established by Medicare. Without an effective process for KX modifier application, claims for PT, OT, and SLP services beyond Medicare's annual limits are denied, creating unnecessary administrative burden and delayed payments for practices.

To help providers stay ahead of modifier requirements, we built an automated KX Modifier assistant directly into Air. This AI-powered automation intelligently applies KX Modifiers to claims that have surpassed Medicare's therapy thresholds, ensuring submissions are complete from the start. By automating this critical but often overlooked step, Air frees providers from the burden of applying KX Modifiers to claims and minimizes undue denials.
