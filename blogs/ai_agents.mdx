Agents Center Overview

Agents Center is your home base for building intelligent assistants that transform manual call flows with scalable automation, integrated directly with Air.

In Air, our Agents have access to all of the following data:

The entire practice scheduling preferences

Patient demographic and clinical history

Insurance eligibility and past claim history

Due to direct, in-built integration into Air, our agents are able to answer the following questions:

Chart Note History: What meds did the doctor last prescribe for me again?

Billing History: Can you tell me how much I owe in my last bill? Why did I get charged that much?

Provider Scheduling Preferences: When is Dr. Bill going on vacation? Can you get me a slot before he goes out?

Comprehensive Inbound Call Management

Agents can handle the full spectrum of patient communications with extreme accuracy.

For Workflows Supporting Existing Patients:

24/7 Rescheduling & Cancellations: We’re able to understand immediately what appointments are upcoming and modify them

Insurance Confirmation: Review insurance info and confirm usage (or collect)

Clarifying Questions: Help patients answer questions related to their previous visits

New Appointments: Schedule new appointments based on patient preferences and booking history

For Workflows Supporting New Patients:

Create the patient profile from scratch

Schedule new appointments

Create cases for each new appointment

Confirm patient eligibility

Outbound Campaigns & Patient Engagement

Agents Center transforms patient engagement through intelligent outbound communications.

Appointment Confirmation: Pre-visit confirmation flow via text or call

Clinical and Post-Op Treatment Plan Follow-Ups: Follow-up based on patient need, and create rules for specific patient populations

Custom Outbound Campaigns: Develop broad rulesets to set up outbound campaigns for every visit and minimize your post-visit administrative work.

Call Tracking & Analytics

Generate actionable insights for practice optimization from your Agents to gain insight into performance and modify Agents as needed.

Real-Time Call Monitoring: Monitor and view calls made by agents as they happen. Review and modify remaining actions.

Automated Transcription and Call Logs: View transcripts and call logs all within a single unified view.

Comprehensive Analytics Dashboard: Review Agent performance across dollars saved, patient sentiment, and other key metrics.
